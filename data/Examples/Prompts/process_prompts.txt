Názov procesu 1:
Exam registration for an available exam date

Doména:
University study administration

Opis procesu:
Actors are Student and AIS. The student logs into AIS and opens the exam registration module. The student selects the course for which they want to register. AIS displays the available exam dates together with date, time, location, and remaining capacity. The student selects one exam date and submits the registration request. AIS checks whether the selected exam date still has free capacity. If the capacity is available, AIS registers the student for the exam and shows a confirmation of successful registration. If the capacity is full, AIS rejects the registration and shows an error message. After the registration result is displayed, the process moves to the common final state Registration request closed.

kategoria K2

Názov procesu 4:
Issuing a confirmation of enrollment

Doména:
University study administration

Opis procesu:
Actors are Student, Study Office Staff, and Student Information System. The student requests a confirmation of enrollment. The request can be submitted electronically through the student information system or in person at the Study Office. In the electronic path, the student fills in and submits an online request form. In the in-person path, Study Office Staff receives the request and enters it into the system on behalf of the student. The Student Information System checks whether the student currently has active enrollment status in a degree program. If the status is active, the system generates a confirmation of enrollment as a PDF document. Study Office Staff then decides whether to print the confirmation or send it to the student by email. The selected delivery method is completed and the student receives the document. If the enrollment status is not active, the system rejects the request and shows a message that the confirmation cannot be issued. After the final result is communicated, the process moves to the common final state Certificate request closed.

Názov procesu 7:
Online semester course registration with prerequisite, timetable, and credit checks

Doména:
University study administration

Opis procesu:
Actors are Student, AIS, Curriculum Records System, Timetable Service, and Study Office. The student logs into AIS. The student opens the online course registration module for the upcoming semester. AIS loads the student degree plan. AIS loads the current academic record. AIS displays the list of available courses for the semester. The student browses the available courses. The student selects a course to add. AIS sends a prerequisite check request to the Curriculum Records System. The Curriculum Records System verifies whether all required prerequisites are satisfied. If the prerequisites are not satisfied, AIS rejects the selected course, shows a prerequisite error message, and returns the student to course selection. If the prerequisites are satisfied, AIS sends a timetable conflict check request to the Timetable Service. The Timetable Service verifies whether the selected course conflicts with already selected or already registered courses. If a timetable conflict exists, AIS rejects the selected course, shows a timetable conflict message, and returns the student to course selection. If no timetable conflict exists, AIS checks whether adding the course would exceed the semester credit limit. If the credit limit would be exceeded, AIS rejects the selected course, shows a credit limit error message, and the student may request assistance from the Study Office. The Study Office reviews the problem and may advise the student to choose a different course. If all checks pass, AIS adds the course to the student registration. The student may continue selecting additional courses. When the student finishes the selection, the student confirms the semester registration. AIS saves the final set of registered courses, shows a confirmation summary, and moves the process to the common final state Semester registration closed.

Online course registration with repeated selection, section adjustment, and study office intervention

University study administration

If no timetable conflict is found, AIS checks whether the new selection would exceed the allowed semester credit load. If the student modifies the cart independently, the flow returns to course selection. If the student cannot resolve the problem independently, the student contacts the Study Office. The Study Office reviews the registration situation and records manual guidance. After the guidance is recorded, the flow returns to course selection. If all checks pass, AIS adds the selected course to the registration cart. The student decides whether to add another course. If the student wants to add another course, the flow returns to course selection. If the student does not want to add another course, the student confirms the final registration. AIS saves the registered courses, generates a confirmation summary, and moves the process to the common final state Semester registration closed.

Názov procesu 10:
Approval of an individual study plan with advisor review, guarantor decision, and record registration

Doména:
University study administration

Opis procesu:
Actors are Student, AIS, Academic Advisor, Program Guarantor, Study Office, and Curriculum Registry. The student logs into AIS. The student opens the individual study plan request module. AIS displays the request form. The student enters the proposed set of courses. The student enters the planned credits per semester. The student enters a short justification. The student submits the request. AIS validates the completeness of the request. If the request is incomplete, AIS shows an error message and returns the request to the student for correction. If the request is complete, AIS forwards the request to the Academic Advisor. The Academic Advisor reviews the proposed plan. The Academic Advisor evaluates whether the plan is realistic and academically acceptable. The Academic Advisor adds comments or suggested changes. The Academic Advisor decides whether to recommend the request. If the Academic Advisor does not recommend the request, AIS notifies the student and returns the request for revision. The student reviews the comments, modifies the plan, and resubmits the request. If the Academic Advisor recommends the request, AIS forwards the request to the Program Guarantor. The Program Guarantor reviews the request and the advisor recommendation. The Program Guarantor decides whether to approve the individual study plan. If the Program Guarantor rejects the request, AIS notifies the student. The student decides whether to create a new request. If the student creates a new request, the flow returns to the request form. If the student does not create a new request, the process moves to the common final state Request closed without approval. If the Program Guarantor approves the request, AIS forwards the decision to the Study Office. The Study Office registers the approved plan in the Curriculum Registry. The Curriculum Registry updates the student study record. AIS sends a confirmation to the student and moves the process to the common final state Individual study plan approved and registered.

Názov procesu 13:
Onboarding of a newly admitted PhD student with enrollment, HR processing, IT activation, and supervisor assignment

Doména:
University doctoral study administration

Opis procesu:
Actors: PhD Student, Study Office, HR Department, IT Department, Department Administration, Supervisor, University Systems.

The process starts when PhD Student receives the admission decision. PhD Student signs the admission decision. PhD Student contacts Study Office and requests onboarding. Study Office opens the onboarding case in University Systems. Study Office checks the required admission documents.

If any required document is missing, Study Office requests the missing documents from PhD Student. PhD Student submits the missing documents. Study Office checks whether the documents were submitted before the deadline. If the deadline is missed, Study Office cancels the onboarding case. University Systems records the cancellation. Study Office informs PhD Student. The process ends.

If all required documents are available, Study Office performs formal enrollment. University Systems creates the doctoral student record. University Systems confirms active enrollment.

After formal enrollment, two branches run in parallel. In the first branch, HR Department opens an employment case. HR Department verifies personal data. HR Department verifies legal data. HR Department prepares the employment contract. HR Department sends the contract to PhD Student. PhD Student reviews the contract. PhD Student decides whether to sign the contract. If the contract is not signed, HR Department records employment failure. HR Department informs Study Office. HR Department informs Supervisor. The process ends. If the contract is signed, HR Department creates the employment relationship in the HR system.

In the second branch, IT Department receives a request for account creation. IT Department checks whether the active person record is available in University Systems. If the person record is missing, IT Department asks Study Office to complete the missing data. Study Office updates the person record in University Systems. IT Department creates the university account. IT Department creates the university email account. IT Department grants basic system access. IT Department activates the account. IT Department confirms working access.

After both branches are completed, Department Administration assigns the workplace. Department Administration assigns Supervisor if not already assigned. Supervisor accepts the supervision role. Department Administration schedules an introductory meeting. Supervisor holds the introductory meeting with PhD Student. Department Administration proposes the planned start date. PhD Student decides whether the start date is acceptable. If the start date is delayed, Department Administration suspends the remaining onboarding steps until the arrival date. Department Administration resumes onboarding after the arrival date.

Department Administration registers the workplace assignment in University Systems. Department Administration registers the supervisor assignment in University Systems. University Systems sends final onboarding confirmation. The process ends.

Supervisor explains working rules. Department Administration proposes the planned start date. PhD Student decides whether the arrival is delayed. If the arrival is delayed, Department Administration pauses selected onboarding tasks until the actual arrival date. Department Administration resumes onboarding after the actual arrival date.

Department Administration records the workplace assignment in University Systems. Department Administration records the supervisor assignment in University Systems. University Systems records completion status. University Systems sends final onboarding confirmation. The process ends.
